About Me

Hello, my name is Paola Jarquin, I am 21 years old and from Oaxaca, Mexico. I feel very grateful for the gospel of Jesus Christ, which gives me happiness and purpose every day. I belong to The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints and had the blessing of serving as a missionary in the Mexico Monterrey East Mission, an experience that strengthened my faith and taught me to love and serve others.
